About Us

A Passionate Kenyan, inspired by the pure love of food, joy and her Grandma's own delicous cooking,risks its all, opens an artisan pizza pie place and names it after her long closed Grandma's Restaurant in hopes to continue the family dream. Its that same dream that gives Momo's Pizza its appeal.A living proof that dreams do come true.

We are built on the dream of healing the world one pizza piece at a time. Therefore you are assured of the best customer service experience in the world.

In our kitchen we make sure that the dishes are prepared only from the highest quality products. And every Pizza sold is prepared with lots of love by some of the best chefs. Thanks to this, our pizza is a delicacy that can not be resisted!
